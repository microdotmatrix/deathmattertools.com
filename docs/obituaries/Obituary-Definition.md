# Obituary Definition

# **Obituary (noun):**

A published notice of a person’s death that includes a summary of their life, accomplishments, relationships, and information about funeral or memorial services.

An obituary is both a death announcement and a tribute, meant to inform the public while preserving the memory of a person’s life.

# **Obituaries are designed to:**

# **Announce the death**

Provides the public with accurate information about someone’s passing. Option to include cause of death. 2. # Honor and memorialize the deceased

Highlights the individual’s personality, achievements, values, and impact on others. 3. # Notify the community

Especially useful for extended family, friends, and community members who may not have been directly informed. 4. # Offer funeral/service details

Shares public information about visitations, services, or memorials. 5. # Provide a historical record

Obituaries become part of the public record and genealogical archives. 6. # Celebrate a life lived

Especially in modern obituaries, there’s often an emphasis on storytelling, legacy, and personal details over formal summaries.
